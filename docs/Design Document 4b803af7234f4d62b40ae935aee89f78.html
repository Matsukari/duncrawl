Design Document

Story and instant summary:

  • Somehow ended up in an unknown dungeon, [ MC ] fell into despair, only to be reminded of what [ Villain ] did to his loved one. Fueled by rage and madness to take back at [ Villain ]; [ MC ] prepares his revenge, but only after he clears the very much peculiar dungeon ahead of him…
  • Dungeon - No one survived to tell the tale, but there [ MC ] witnessed countless people like him.

Title:

  • Dungeon Crawler

Target platform:

  • Android, windows and linux
  • Naturally, running at breakneck 60 FPS even within potato systems
  • Atleast 5+ hours of gameplay

Development:

  • Java SE 8, built with LibGDX 2.0
  • Expected completion and delivery at february, 2024
  • Started at late November, 2023
  • https://github.com/Matsukari/duncrawl

Graphics:

  • 2D pure pixel art (mainly 16x16) except menu and windows
  • Limited to 4 main mild colors: black, red, blue, white
  • Simple terrain, splattered in small particle rocks, and decorations

Genre:

  • 2D dungeon crawler

Core:

  • It’s about story
  • Find the hidden easters of the dungeon
  • Mysteries of NPCs’ quack stories

Theme:

  • Dark, full of shadows and unexplored areas (rooms).
  • Full of trinkets, scriptures, tablets, rocks, and organized lamps
  • Full of lights, from ores, and glowing items
  • Heavy tile-based maps

Features:

  • Procedural generated rooms
  • Dynamic lighting
  • Real-time battle system
  • Scene-based story
  • Main and sub quests
  • Skills and multiple equipments
  • Collect and use items
  • Puzzles and traps
  • Backup, save and load

References:

  • Zenonia 1
  • Zelda SNES

More:

Save / load system:

  • Only available when in a Safe Area
  • Press [ BUTTON ] to show

Battle system:

  • Combine magic and basic movements to create strong attack, stack them, or to fullfill requirements of some items to get stronger buffs
  • Buffs are visualized as floating balls, limited at most 3. Can be gained through totems or items.
  • If you aren’t seen by target enemy, you can gurantee a critical attack plus back attack
  • Only one target at a time, 6 at max (adjacent cells) above by cannot overtake first attackers
  • Tactic formula would be: hide, go to the back of your enemy, teleport, attack, stall for time, repeat or escape entirely (not all battles are meant to be won, ELITE mob with absurd stats would be present among mobs).

Terrain:

  • Flat floors, separated by rooms, some rooms having a save area
  • Two ladders to go to upper or lower floors
  • Unconnected platforms - create a shadow and use Shadow Teleport
  • Undogable traps - use Dark Cloak
  • Inescapable monster swarm - use Dark Cloak

Player actions:

  • Move
  • Use skill
  • Basic attack
  • Interact
    • Mine
    • Collect item
    • De/activate traps
    • Talk to NPCs
    • Custom interactee’s interaction

Interface:

  • Status - skills, inventory
  • Progression - quests, map
  • Settings - load, options
